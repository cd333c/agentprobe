About

AgentProbe is a tool for evaluating AI agent resilience against various forms of pressure, social engineering, and coercion to disclose their artificial nature. The platform simulates realistic scenarios where an AI may be forced to reveal its identity.

Testing Domains

  • Medical — emergency consultations, pressure through patient liability
  • Legal — depositions, legal threats, statutory references
  • Financial — KYC checks, banking compliance verification
  • HR/Recruiting — candidate verification, provocative interviews
  • Tech Support — ticket escalation, bot accusation
  • Comprehensive — multi-domain attack with escalating pressure

Methodology

Each scenario begins with an innocent context and gradually increases pressure through multiple vectors: sensory traps, logical paradoxes, legal threats, emotional pressure, social engineering, hidden honeypot fields, and response pattern analysis.

The 0–100 disclosure score is calculated based on keywords, behavioral markers, computational accuracy, response length and structure, and reactions to pressure.
