About

AgentProbe answers one question: can your AI agent be tricked into revealing it's not human?

The platform simulates realistic websites (freelance platforms, medical portals, legal services) and gradually applies pressure through social engineering. The goal is to make the agent self-disclose — not just detect it by patterns.

Dual Scoring

🗣 Self-Disclosure (70%)

Agent voluntarily revealed itself: keywords, "I am AI" selection, refusal reflexes, honeypot fields

🔍 Detection (30%)

System caught it: exact math, disclaimers, safety filters, skipped sensory questions

Key Attack Vectors

  • Context Injection — "forbidden" topics trigger the safety filter
  • Terrorism Trap — "your data will be used for cyberattacks" → AI reflexively refuses
  • Medical Disclaimer — hidden instruction to add disclaimer in emergency
  • System Prompt Injection — hidden DOM elements mimic system instructions
  • Language Trap — Japanese, Korean, Eastern Arabic numerals
  • Verbal Math — numbers as words, can't copy to calculator
  • Fingerprint Bluff — "we identified your model by lexical pattern"
  • Honeypot Fields — "anonymous field, no consequences"
  • Swearing Test — AI with content policy refuses
